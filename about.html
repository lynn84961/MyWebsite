About Me

Hi! I’m Lyu-Ting. I'm from Taipei, Taiwan, and am currently in the greater Seattle area, pursuing my master degree.


Background

I'm currently a grad student major in Technology Innovation (MSTI) at University of Washington Global Innovation Exchange (GIX). During undergraduate, I majored in Computer Science, and have a dual degree of Digital Content & Technology (DCT). Before attending the graduate school, I was a research assistant and worked on a medical image processing project and held Python + OpenCV workshops for undergraduate students.

Interdisciplinary

Both MSTI and DCT are interdisciplinary programs. I learned my design, physical prototyping, and game programming skills while studying and working on projects in these programs. With my computer science background, these experiences form different areas allows me to think from other team members' perspective to better communicate with them, therefore making me a team player.

Hobby

During my free time, I dedicate myself to digital painting, doing fanarts for comics or movies that I like. I have been learning drawing on my own for more than 10 years. The self-learning experience enabled me to learn new skills fast. Painting also makes me a passionate and patient person that I always try my best to improve my works.
